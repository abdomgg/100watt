Purchase Order Approval Workflow

This module adds a complete approval workflow for purchase orders, ensuring proper authorization before confirming purchases. Configure approvers for each buyer, track approval status, and maintain full control over your procurement process with automated notifications and activity tracking.

Main Features

  • Configure approvers for each user in user settings
  • Automatic approval request when purchase order is created or confirmed
  • Track approval status with pending/approved/rejected states
  • Send approval activities to designated approvers
  • Multiple approvers support with sequence ordering
  • Approve or reject purchase orders with one click
  • Automatic purchase order confirmation after approval
  • Full integration with Odoo Purchase and Mail modules

Configure Approvers

Set up approvers for each buyer in the user settings. Define who needs to approve purchase orders created by specific users. You can add multiple approvers with sequence ordering to control the approval flow.

Purchase Order Pending Approval

When a buyer creates or confirms a purchase order, it automatically enters the "Pending Approval" state. The purchase order cannot be confirmed until an approver reviews and approves it. Approvers receive activities in their dashboard for quick action.

Approve or Reject

Approvers can easily approve or reject purchase orders with dedicated buttons. The approval action automatically confirms the purchase order, while rejection returns it to draft state for modifications. All actions are tracked and logged.

Approval Activities

Approvers receive automatic activities in their dashboard when purchase orders need approval. Activities include purchase order details and direct links to the order, making the approval process fast and efficient.

Approval Status Tracking

Track the approval status of each purchase order with clear status indicators. See who has approved, who is pending, and the complete approval history. The approval state is tracked separately from the purchase order state for better visibility.

Easy Setup & User-Friendly

  • Simple configuration in user settings
  • Automatic approval workflow activation
  • Clear approval buttons and status indicators
  • Activity-based notifications for approvers
  • Full audit trail of all approval actions

Why Use This Module?

This module ensures proper authorization and control over your procurement process. Prevent unauthorized purchases, maintain budget control, and ensure compliance with your company's approval policies. Perfect for businesses that need multi-level approval workflows for purchase orders.
